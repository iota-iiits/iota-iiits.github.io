About Us

IOTA is a technical club of IIIT, Sri City. Our members regularly work on projects ranging from IoT, AI, AR, Application/Web Development and Cloud with the help of faculty mentors.
We have collaborated with other organizations from different domains to work on projects or problem statements. We take up projects or try to work on problem statements.
We also have study groups where students work and learn together. We also keep on conducting hackathons, workshops and tutorial sessions regularly.
